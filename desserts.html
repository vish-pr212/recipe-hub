Dessert Recipes

1. Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Whisk eggs, sugar, butter, and vanilla in another bowl.
  4. Combine both mixtures to form batter.
  5. Bake for 25–30 mins and let cool before serving.

2. Vanilla Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Mix sugar, cornstarch, and salt in a saucepan.
  2. Slowly whisk in milk until smooth.
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until thickened.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Chill for 2 hours before serving.
