Appetizer Recipes

1. Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp chopped parsley
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix butter, garlic, and parsley.
  3. Spread mixture on bread slices.
  4. Bake for 5–7 minutes until golden.

2. Vegetable Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 6 spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Sauté cabbage and carrots in oil.
  2. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  3. Fill wrappers, roll tightly.
  4. Deep-fry or bake until golden brown.
